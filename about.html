Our Mission

At Synapse Symphony, we are redefining chronic pain care through advanced AI powered, noninvasive neurostimulation. Sparked by a powerful moment when a patient in our early research said she felt relief for the first time in twenty years, we knew we had to bring this breakthrough beyond the lab and into people’s lives.

How We Work

Field shaping with precision

Our platform combines adaptive high density brain stimulation with AI algorithms that personalize therapy based on each patient’s neuroanatomy, symptoms, and response patterns. We use biophysical modeling to sculpt electric fields generated in the brain through the scalp, enabling precise modulation of pain related circuits without surgery or clinical oversight.

Comfort that sustains therapy

A key differentiator is our background hum technique. Traditional systems can cause local pain or skin irritation from current injection at the scalp. By reducing these sensations, background hum improves tolerability and compliance, making therapy better suited for daily, at home use.

Always learning

By continuously learning from patient data, the system intelligently adjusts stimulation to keep relief high while discomfort stays low. The objective is consistent, dependable therapy that fits real life.

From lab insight to patient impact

We translate neuroscience and clinical insight into a product pathway that puts people first. The focus is on comfort, personalization, and measurable improvement in daily function.
