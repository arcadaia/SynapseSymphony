Our approach

At Synapse Symphony, we are redefining chronic pain care with AI powered, noninvasive neurostimulation. The work began with a simple idea. Bring meaningful relief beyond the lab and into daily life.

Our platform combines adaptive high density stimulation with algorithms that personalize therapy using each personâ€™s neuroanatomy, symptoms, and response patterns. Field shaping through the scalp enables precise modulation without surgery or clinic visits.

The background hum innovation aims to lower the discomfort that can occur with current injection at the scalp. The goal is better tolerability and better adherence so people can complete effective sessions at home.

By learning continuously from patient data, the system adjusts to maximize relief while minimizing discomfort. This is how we move from one size fits all to truly personal therapy.
