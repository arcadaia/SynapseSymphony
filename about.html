About

Redefining Chronic Pain Care

Advanced AI powered, noninvasive neurostimulation for daily at home relief.

Our Mission

At Synapse Symphony, we are redefining chronic pain care through advanced AI powered, noninvasive neurostimulation. Sparked by a powerful moment when a patient in our early research said she felt relief for the first time in twenty years, we knew we had to bring this breakthrough beyond the lab and into people’s lives.

What guides our work

Personalized modulation

Adaptive high density stimulation shaped to neuroanatomy, symptoms, and response patterns.

Comfort by design

Background hum reduces discomfort from current injection and supports daily use at home.

Learning system

Continuous adjustment using patient data to sustain relief while minimizing discomfort.

How we work

Field shaping with precision

Our platform combines adaptive high density stimulation with algorithms that personalize therapy using each person’s neuroanatomy, symptoms, and response patterns. We use biophysical modeling to sculpt fields through the scalp to modulate pain related circuits precisely, without surgery or clinic visits.

Comfort that sustains therapy

Traditional systems can cause local pain or irritation from current injection at the scalp. Our background hum aims to lower these sensations and improve session tolerability so people can complete effective therapy at home.

Always learning

The system learns continuously from patient data and adjusts stimulation patterns to keep relief high while burden stays low. The objective is dependable therapy that fits daily life.

From lab insight to impact

We translate neuroscience and clinical insight into a product pathway that puts people first with comfort, personalization, and measurable improvement in function.

Questions

QIs the therapy surgical?

No. It is noninvasive and designed for at-home use with clinical guidance as needed.

QHow long is a typical session?

Session length can vary by protocol. The system is designed to support comfortable, repeatable sessions suitable for daily use.

QWhat does “background hum” feel like?

It’s a comfort technique intended to reduce the sharp tingling that can occur with current injection at the scalp, helping sessions feel smoother.

QWho is this for?

Our early work focuses on chronic pain conditions such as fibromyalgia and osteoarthritis. Suitability depends on individual evaluation.

QIs it FDA approved?

We are operating in an MVP stage using cleared components while building evidence and advancing toward an integrated home device.

QCan it be used with medications or physical therapy?

Many pain management plans are multimodal. Therapy choices should be made with a clinician who knows your medical history.

QHow does personalization work?

High-density arrays and field shaping are guided by models and patient data to target pain-related circuits more precisely.

QWhat about data privacy?

Patient data is handled with strict privacy and security standards. Only data necessary for personalization is used.

QWhen will it be broadly available?

We are building evidence and product maturity. Timelines depend on study results, regulatory pathways, and manufacturing.

QIs it covered by insurance?

Coverage varies by plan and indication. We will share updates on access pathways as we progress.
