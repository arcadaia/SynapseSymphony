About

At Synapse Symphony, we are redefining chronic pain care through advanced AI-powered, non-invasive neurostimulation. Sparked by a powerful moment when a patient in our early research said she felt relief for the first time in 20 years, we knew we had to bring this breakthrough beyond the lab and into people's lives.

Our proprietary platform combines adaptive high-density brain stimulation with AI algorithms that personalize treatment based on each patient’s neuroanatomy, symptoms, and response patterns. We leverage proprietary algorithms and biophysical modeling to sculpt electric fields generated in the brain through the scalp, allowing for precise modulation of pain-related brain circuits without surgery or clinical oversight.

Another key differentiator is our novel “background hum” technique, which reduces discomfort caused by injected currents—a common issue in conventional stimulation systems that often leads to local pain or skin irritation. By minimizing these adverse sensations, background hums significantly improve patient tolerability and compliance, making our solution better suited for daily, at-home use.

By continuously learning from patient data, our system intelligently adjusts stimulation to maximize relief while minimizing discomfort.
