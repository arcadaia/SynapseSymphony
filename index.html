About

At Synapse Symphony, we are redefining chronic pain care through advanced AI-powered, non-invasive neurostimulation. Sparked by a powerful moment when a patient in our early research said she felt relief for the first time in 20 years, we knew we had to bring this breakthrough beyond the lab and into people's lives.

Our proprietary platform combines adaptive high-density brain stimulation with AI algorithms that personalize treatment based on each patient’s neuroanatomy, symptoms, and response patterns.
