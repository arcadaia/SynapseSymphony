About

At Synapse Symphony, we’re revolutionizing chronic pain treatment through intelligent, patient-tailored brain stimulation. Our journey began when one patient, after decades of suffering, finally felt true relief — and we knew it had to go further than the lab.

We combine adaptive high-density neurostimulation with proprietary AI algorithms that continuously learn from each user’s anatomy and symptoms. This approach delivers precise, comfortable, and self-adjusting care — at home, every day.

Our unique "background hum" technique reduces discomfort caused by current injection, addressing a major pain point in traditional stimulation systems. The result? More relief, fewer side effects, and a system people actually want to use.
