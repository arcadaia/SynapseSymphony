Meet the People Behind Synapse Symphony

Engineering AI-guided, non-invasive neurostimulation with comfort, precision, and purpose.

Board Members

Pulkit Grover, Ph.D.

Co-Founder & CEO

Dr. Pulkit Grover (Co-Founder & CEO) is a Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University specializing in brain stimulation, AI/ML, and systems theory. He leads vision, product strategy, and algorithm development, drawing on a decade of pioneering work in non-invasive neuromodulation.

Vishal Jain, Ph.D.

Co-Founder & COO

Dr. Vishal Jain (Co-Founder & COO) is a research scientist with a strong track record in in-vivo neuroscience, system integration, and clinical validation. He leads clinical operations, regulatory planning, and hardware-software translation, including coordination of IRB-approved pilot studies.
Clinical Advisor

Benedict Alter, MD, Ph.D.

Co-Founder & Clinical Advisor

Dr. Benedict Alter (Co-Founder & Clinical Advisor) is an MD/PhD in anesthesiology and pain medicine at the University of Pittsburgh. He oversees pilot trials in fibromyalgia and osteoarthritis, guiding protocol design, patient selection, and therapeutic response.
