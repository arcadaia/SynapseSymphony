Meet the People Building Synapse Symphony

Pulkit Grover, Ph.D.

Co-Founder & CEO

Dr. Pulkit Grover is a Professor of Electrical & Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. He leads vision, product strategy, and algorithms, drawing on a decade of pioneering research in non-invasive neuromodulation, AI/ML, and systems theory.

Vishal Jain, Ph.D.

Dr. Vishal Jain is a research scientist with a strong record in in-vivo neuroscience, system integration, and clinical validation. He leads clinical operations, regulatory planning, and hardware-software translation including IRB-approved pilot studies.
