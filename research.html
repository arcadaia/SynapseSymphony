Technology

Reimagining Pain Relief Through Intelligent Neurostimulation

We are developing noninvasive neuromodulation that delivers personalized, effective relief by targeting brain pain circuits with precision and comfort.

Personalized Brain Modulation

Platform

Targeted stimulation shaped to each person

Our platform uses high density electrode arrays combined with adaptive electric field shaping to tailor stimulation to individual neuroanatomy and pain profile.

This personalized approach aims to provide more effective and longer lasting relief compared to conventional therapies.

The Science Behind the Relief

Evidence

Smarter and more responsive therapy

Built on years of neuroscience and clinical insight, the system adjusts stimulation patterns in real time using patient specific data for a safer and more responsive experience.

Early pilot work in fibromyalgia and osteoarthritis has shown strong potential with reported improvement in mobility and reduction in pain.

Breakthrough: Background Hum

Comfort

Reduce discomfort and support longer sessions

Traditional electrical stimulation can cause scalp discomfort due to current injection. Our background hum technique aims to reduce this sensation and improve session tolerability.

The goal is consistent therapeutic dosing without distress so people can complete longer home sessions comfortably.

From Prototype to Product

Roadmap

Faster access today and a clear path to home use

We are operating in an MVP stage using a modular setup built from cleared components to validate outcomes while expanding access for patients.

Next steps include miniaturization and integration into a compact device designed for daily use at home.
